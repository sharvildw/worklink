Privacy Policy

How WorkLink stores and uses account and platform data.

WorkLink stores profile, job, and contact details that are required to operate the marketplace experience during testing and presentation.

Information such as user names, emails, worker skills, locations, and posted job details may be used to demonstrate platform workflows and dashboard features.

Sensitive or unnecessary personal data should not be entered into the system because this project is intended for educational use and demonstration.
